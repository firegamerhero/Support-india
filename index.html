Our Mission

Who We Are

Support India Initiative ek social initiative hai jiska aim hai zaruratmand logon ki madad karna — chahe wo ghar banana ho, khana provide karna ho, education support ho ya medical help.

Hum verified cases ke liye campaigns chalate hain jahan har ek donation directly needy logon tak pahunchta hai. Based in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Transparency & Trust

Legal Information

Hum poori transparency ke saath kaam karte hain. Yahan hamare policies aur terms hain.

🔐 Privacy Policy

Support India Initiative aapki personal information ko secure rakhti hai. Hamari privacy policy ke baare mein:

  • Donor ka personal data (naam, contact) bina permission ke kisi ke saath share nahi kiya jayega.
  • Payments sirf trusted UPI methods ke through liye jaate hain — PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM.
  • Website par diya gaya data sirf donation processing aur communication ke liye use hota hai.
  • Hum aapke data ko sell, trade, ya third parties ko transfer nahi karte.
  • Certificate generation ke liye diya gaya naam/amount sirf aapke certificate mein use hoga.
  • Koi bhi data query ke liye: supportindiainitiative@gmail.com par contact karein.

📜 Terms & Conditions

  • Sabhi donations completely voluntary hain — koi compulsion nahi hai.
  • Donated amount non-refundable hoga in normal circumstances.
  • Funds sirf social welfare campaigns (ghar, khana, education, medical) ke liye use honge.
  • Organisation complete transparency maintain karega aur regular updates share karega.
  • Har campaign mein progress updates donors ke saath share ki jaayengi.
  • Minimum donation ₹1 se start hoti hai — chhoti se chhoti madad bhi accept ki jaati hai.
  • Donation certificate sirf information purposes ke liye hai.

⚠️ Disclaimer

  • Support India Initiative ek independent social initiative hai — registered NGO nahi hai abhi.
  • Sabhi cases verify karne ki puri koshish ki jaati hai before accepting donations.
  • Donors apni ichha se contribution karte hain — kisi pressure mein nahi.
  • Hum guarantee nahi de sakte ki 100% goal hamesha achieve hoga, lekin poori koshish ki jaati hai.
